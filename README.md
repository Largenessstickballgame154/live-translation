## 📌 What is live-translation?

This software adds live subtitles to your screen. It listens to your computer audio and translates speech in real time. It works entirely on your computer without sending data to the internet. You use it to watch videos, join meetings, or play games in languages you do not speak.

The tool uses two parts to function. First, it converts your audio into text. Second, it translates that text into your preferred language. A glass overlay shows these words over your open windows. You see the translation while you work or watch media.

## ⚙️ How it works

The program runs locally on your hardware. It does not need a cloud connection. Because it runs on your machine, your private data remains your own. It uses the power of your processor to handle complex language tasks. You do not need to pay for extra translation services or monthly subscriptions.

## 🛠️ Setting up the app

When you open the app for the first time, you see a small window. This window contains your primary settings.

1. **Input Device**: Choose the audio source for the app. Select your microphone or the "Stereo Mix" option to capture system sounds.
2. **Target Language**: Pick the language you want to see on your screen.
3. **Source Language**: Pick the language of the speaker. You can also select "Auto-Detect" to let the app guess the language.
4. **Overlay**: Turn on the overlay to see the text box. Drag the box to any position on your screen. You can change the size of the font or the background color inside the settings menu.

## 📝 Tips for the best results

Good audio quality makes the translation accurate. If the app struggles to understand the speech, check the following items.

*   **Background Noise**: Keep your microphone away from fans or loud environment noises.
*   **Proximity**: Sit close to your microphone when you speak.
*   **Clarity**: Speak at a steady pace. Fast speech or overlapping voices confuse the transcription engine.
*   **Language Selection**: If you already know the input language, select it manually instead of using "Auto-Detect". This improves speed and precision.
